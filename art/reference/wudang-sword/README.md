# 武當劍法大要 reference plates (1931)

Source: Huang Yuanxiu 黃元秀, "Essentials of the Wudang Sword Art" (武當劍法大要),
Commercial Press Shanghai, 1931 — the Li Jinglin 李景林 Wudang sword lineage.
Scraped from Paul Brennan's translation post (brennantranslation.wordpress.com/
2014/06/01/wudang-sword/), which reproduces the original plates. The 1931
photographs/diagrams are public domain; Brennan's English text is his — we use
only the plates + the original Chinese, as ART REFERENCE for Hu's combat
keyframes (f1 stance, attack/block keys).

Contents: #001-008 calligraphy front matter (incl. Li Jinglin's 身如遊龍 preface)
· #009-011 portraits · #012-052 the paired-form technique photographs
(十三勢: 抽帶提格 擊刺點崩 攪壓劈截洗) · #053-055 yin-yang grip-clock diagrams
(太陰/中陰/老陰/少陽 hand positions) · #056-058 sword anatomy.

The 13 techniques (十三字訣): 抽 chou draw-cut · 帶 dai drag-cut · 提 ti lift ·
格 ge block/deflect · 擊 ji strike · 刺 ci thrust · 點 dian wrist-point ·
崩 beng upward flick · 攪 jiao stir/coil · 壓 ya press · 劈 pi chop ·
截 jie intercept · 洗 xi washing cut. Lead hand = 戟指 "sword fingers,"
half-circle arc. 練劍之要 身如遊龍 切忌停滯 — "the body like a swimming dragon,
never stagnating."
