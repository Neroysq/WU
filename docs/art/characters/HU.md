# Hu (胡) — Character Design

**Date:** 2026-07-03 · **Status:** concept round 1 — awaiting user pick
**Sources:** identity spec (`2026-07-03-creative-identity-revamp-design.md` §3), ART_DESIGN_DOC v2.1 (§2, §4, §5b), canon plan Task 5.

## 1. Who he is (design must carry this)

- **The starstruck pilgrim.** A young nobody who climbed 九仙山 to *learn* — raised on tales of the Nine, hungry for a master and an autograph. Not chosen, not talented-born: **enthusiastic**. His personality lives in reaction frames; his tragedy in the corruption stages.
- **The 胡/虎 pun.** His name sounds like "Tiger" — one of the missing famous styles. The design must NOT look tiger-marked (the joke works because he looks like *nobody*); at most a private wink (e.g. a cheap tiger charm from a souvenir stall — he's a fan).
- **Nobody's disciple.** No school colors, no sect marks, no style-signature in his idle. His stance is honest and slightly wrong — self-taught from watching.
